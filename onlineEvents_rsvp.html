About the Position

CercaLabs has developed a number of software products in the healthcare, transportation and hospitality industries. We provide enterprise quality web-based and mobile solutions for our clients. This role reports to the Managing Director and supports the growth and expansion of the business.

Essential Job Functions

The UX Designer will focus on designing UX for web and mobile applications for CercaLabs clients. Projects will primarily focus on enterprise workflow applications where clean design and usability are key to improving our clients' productivity.

This is a project based position and we are looking for someone with experience in enterprise application design, attention to detail, an inquisitive mind and technical curiosity.

Above all else, we are looking for candidates with a great attitude and discipline to complete their projects on-time and accustomed to agile development methodology.

Job Responsibilities

Works with Product Management resources in translating product requirements to design features. . Create storyboards, wireframes, and high fidelity mockups and prototypes to support product requirements and sales demo needs. Qualifications and Skills

Essential Qualifications (Skills, Background and Education)

Software design or development experience within a professional organization.
Excellent problem-solving skills.
Technical curiosity and attention to detail.
Experience web and mobile UX design focused on transaction efficiency.
Excellent written, verbal and phone communication skills.
Excellent team player.
Degree in Software, Engineering or Technical disciplines.
